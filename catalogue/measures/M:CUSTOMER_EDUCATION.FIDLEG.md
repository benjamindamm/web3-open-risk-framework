# Customer Education Program (FIDLEG Compliant)

## Measure Description

This measure implements a comprehensive customer education program to inform clients about Web3 risks, DeFi protocols, and regulatory protections under Swiss FIDLEG Article 14 client protection obligations.

## FIDLEG Compliance Context

### Regulatory Classification
- **FIDLEG Article**: Art. 14 (Client Protection)
- **Implementation Deadline**: 2024-03-31
- **Audit Frequency**: ANNUAL review required
- **Regulatory Requirement**: MANDATORY for Swiss market access
- **Mitigation Type**: ORGANIZATIONAL implementation

### Swiss Market Requirements
- Comprehensive client education materials
- Annual review and update of educational content
- PDF documentation for regulatory compliance
- Client acknowledgment and understanding verification

- Risk education material creation

## Educational Content

### Core Modules
- **Web3 Fundamentals**: Blockchain basics, wallet security, transaction types
- **DeFi Risks**: Smart contract risks, liquidity risks, governance risks
- **Regulatory Protections**: Swiss client protection, FINMA oversight, dispute resolution
- **Best Practices**: Security measures, risk management, due diligence

### Documentation Requirements
- **PDF Format**: All educational materials in PDF format
- **Digital Signatures**: Client acknowledgment and completion certificates
- **Audit Trail**: Complete learning progress documentation
- **Regulatory Compliance**: FINMA-approved content and format
